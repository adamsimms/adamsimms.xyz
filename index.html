Adam Simms

Technologist, designer, artist, and educator

I lead, mentor, and implement technology visions, transform strategy into prototypes, and build delightful experiences.

I've worked as a design contributor, director, and leader at Trello, Atlassian, Lightspeed, and Age of Union.

I was an Artist in Residence (AiR) at Concordia University, where I've taught As Seen on Screens: Cyberspace & Image Economy (PHOT 398), Digital Photography I (PHOT 331), Digital Photography II (PHOT 332), and Advanced Workshop (PHOT 400) in the Photography Program of the Department of Studio Arts.

I create immersive experiences that combine photography, new media, and microelectronics to explore the preservation of intangible culture.

I'm the cofounder of RECESS, a wellness club in Montreal.

/ I hold an MFA in Photography and BFA in Design from Concordia University

/ I'm a Post-Image Cluster research member within the Milieux Institute for Arts, Culture and Technology.
